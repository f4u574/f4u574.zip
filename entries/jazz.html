What if writing is not writing but composing? Not in the musical sense exclusively but in the sense that John Cage meant — organizing sounds, and silences, and the accidents in between.

I compose in text the way a jazz musician composes: with a structure loose enough to allow improvisation, with repetition that isn't repetition because the context has shifted, with space for the unexpected to enter and rearrange everything.

This entry itself is a composition. Text and image and (soon) sound, not illustrating each other but coexisting.
