rhizome not a tree
🌿 growing · text
03 18

Deleuze and Guattari described a model of knowledge that has no hierarchy, no center, no beginning or end. Any point can connect to any other point.

I've been frustrated my entire life by the fact that the world only gives me trees when what I need is a rhizome. Every filing system, every app, every institution — they all want me to put things in branches.

The system I'm building refuses the tree. One database. No folders. No nesting. Just entries with facets — and every entry can be a visitor in multiple rooms simultaneously.

the weight of the knowledge does not weigh me down. and that's why I need systems I could easily float in this space.
